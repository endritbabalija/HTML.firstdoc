Black Holes


First Black Hole image

The Event Horizon Telescope a planet-scale array of ground-based radio telescopes has
obtained the first image of a supermassive black hole and its shadow. The image reveals the
central black hole of Messier 87, a massive galaxy in the Virgo cluster.
